People say a lot about markets — chart signals, timing rules, “buy the dip.” We check each claim against decades of real history, with the same fixed test for every one, and show what actually happened — including when it failed. We never tell you what to buy. Free, forever, selling nothing.

Why trust it? Every claim links to its evidence, and every test uses the same public scoring rules — win or lose, we show the result.

🪞 The mirror — our self-check ▾ tap to open
What "the mirror" means (it ends every room): after testing everyone else's claims, we turn the same test on ourselves — in two words: self-check. Three questions, every time: what did this page actually prove, what can it NOT know, and did it ever slip from showing you what IS into telling you what to DO? A page anywhere else that never holds a mirror to itself is telling you something too.
The mirror, on the front door: free in both directions, forever. It shows what's true, teaches you to check it — including on us — and leaves every decision where it belongs. If this site ever asks for money, keys, or belief, it isn't this site anymore.
